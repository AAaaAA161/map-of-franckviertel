Interface

Controls

Layer Toggles
Click any layer button to show or hide it. Parent layers cascade to all their children. Active layers are highlighted; inactive layers are dimmed.
Focus Mode
Hover over a parent layer to reveal the ◎ focus button. Click it to isolate that layer and all its children — all others are hidden. Click again to exit.
2D / 3D Toggle
Switch between a satellite aerial view (2D) and a tilted vector map with extruded buildings (3D). Located in the bottom-right corner.
Fullscreen
Expand the map to fill the entire screen. Click the icon again (or press Esc) to return to the normal view. Located in the bottom-right corner.
Map Pins & Popups
Click any marker or highlighted area to open a popup with its title, description, and images. The map automatically pans to keep the popup in view. Click the × button or anywhere on the map to close it.
